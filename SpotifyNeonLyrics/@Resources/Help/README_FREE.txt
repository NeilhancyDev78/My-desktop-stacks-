SpotifyNeonLyrics — Free Version Help

Quick start
1) Install WebNowPlaying and make sure your player is supported.
2) Load SpotifyNeonLyrics\Hub.ini
3) Pick Theme, Size, Corners, then click Apply.

Troubleshooting
- No lyrics found: not all tracks have synced lyrics available. Try another song.
- Mojibake / ?????: save INI/Lua as UTF-16 LE (or UTF-8 with BOM).
- Skin won't resize: refresh Main.ini from Rainmeter, or click Apply again.
